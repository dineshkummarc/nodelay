Nodelay!

This page shows recent edits to the English language wikipedia, right now.

What / Why / How?

You're looking at team Nodelay's entry into Node Knockout, a 48hr programming contest to explore node.js.

We're using Jerk, node-websocket-server and node-static so far. Data is streaming from Wikipedia's public IRC feed of recent changes. We borrowed a bit of Pircbot to strip formatting information.
